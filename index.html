Welcome to our Autism Accessibility Training Guide

  • Autism is a condition which affects how a person experiences the world around them. Autistic people see, hear and feel the world in a different way to other people.

  • Although autistic traits vary between individuals, many autistic people experience difficult accessing public spaces.

  • With this website, we hope to raise awareness of how public spaces can be made more accessible using our example of a trip to a museum.
